About the Data

PatentWorld analyzes every patent granted by the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) from 1976 to 2025. The data comes from PatentsView, a platform supported by the USPTO that disambiguates and links patent data. Our analysis covers 9.36 million US patents, their inventors, assignees, technology classifications, geographic origins, and citation networks.
