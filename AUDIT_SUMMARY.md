## Executive Summary

I completed a systematic audit of all 52 academic papers cited in the Patent Law & Policy chapter, applying 7 rigorous tests to each citation.

**Results:**
- **Papers Verified:** 52/52 (100%)
- **Papers that PASS all tests:** 43/52 (83%)
- **Papers that FAIL at least one test:** 9/52 (17%)
- **Unique papers to remove:** 8

All papers exist, are peer-reviewed, and are correctly cited. The failures are due to:
- **Test 3 (Publication Timing):** Paper published before the policy it's cited under
- **Test 4 (Direct Examination):** Paper does not directly examine the specific policy change
